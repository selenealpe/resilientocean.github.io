Resilient Ocean — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

At Resilient Ocean, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our website (www.resilientocean.com), purchase our products, or engage with our services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, institution name, institution type, billing and payment details (processed via secure third-party providers).
  • Account Information: Login credentials, purchase history, saved cart items.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, and usage data (through cookies and analytics tools).
  • Communications: Emails, form submissions, and messages sent to us.

2. How We Collect Your Information

  • Directly from you when you fill in forms, sign up for an account, or purchase a product.
  • Automatically through cookies and analytics tools when you browse our site.
  • From third-party services we integrate with (e.g., payment processors, EmailJS for email automation).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your orders, deliver products, and provide services.
  • To manage your account and saved cart.
  • To send you purchase confirmations, welcome emails, and updates.
  • To improve our website, offerings, and customer support.
  • To meet legal obligations.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we process your personal data based on one or more of the following legal grounds:

  • Your consent (e.g., for marketing emails).
  • Performance of a contract (e.g., delivering a purchased product).
  • Compliance with legal obligations.
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., site security, business analytics).

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to remember your preferences, maintain your shopping cart, and analyze site usage. You can manage cookies through your browser settings, but some features may not work without them. See our Cookies Policy for details.

6. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with:

  • Service providers (e.g., payment processors, email delivery services).
  • Professional advisors (e.g., accountants, legal advisors) where necessary.
  • Authorities, if required by law.

7. Data Retention

We keep your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

8. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
  • Object to processing or request restrictions.
  • Request data portability.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent).
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@resilientocean.com.

9. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. International Transfers

Your data may be stored or processed in countries outside your own. Where required, we ensure appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) are in place.

11. Third-Party Links

Our website may link to third-party sites. We are not responsible for their privacy policies or practices.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last Updated” date.
